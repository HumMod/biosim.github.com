Normal Values > Body Mass

Male

HumMod assumes a typical male body mass of 75 kG (165 Lbs). This value is a little larger than some published values, but people are bigger these days.

Female

Stoudt etal reported an average body mass of 71.7 +/- 10.0 kG for males and 56.7 +/- 8.6 kG for females. These values yield a gender scaling factor of 0.79.

Williams and Leggett suggest 73 kG for males and 60 kG for females. These values yield a gender scaling factor of 0.82.

Taking the average yields 0.806. Scaling down from our 75 kG male yields a typical female body mass of 60.5 kG (133 Lbs).

References
