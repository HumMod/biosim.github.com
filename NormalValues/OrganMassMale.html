Scaling Organ Data

Our scaling algorithm uses the concept of base organ mass. Basic organ mass is the sum of all organ masses except adipose tissue and skeletal muscle. Base organ mass is determined by body size. Skeletal muscle mass and adipose tissue mass are determined by adiposity and muscularity. Values for a typical middle-aged male are tabulated below.
