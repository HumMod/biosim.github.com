<topicdoc>
<name> Gain - PO4P2Definition </name>
<label> Definition </label>
<content>

Gain is defined as positive changes in mass of phosphate
and is a function of dietary intake through GI lumen
absorption.

</content>
</topicdoc>

<topicdoc>
<name> InitialMass - PO4P3Definition </name>
<label> Definition </label>
<content>

The initial mass is the starting amount of phosphate. Since the
plasma concentration is (the Target[PO4--]) and the
volume of distribution is the ECFV (15,000mL), then the
initial mass or circulating pool is .

Initial Mass = Target[PO4--]*ECFV.Vol

</content>
</topicdoc>

<topicdoc>
<name> Loss - PO4P4Definition </name>
<label> Definition </label>
<content>

Loss is defined as negative changes in the mass of phosphate. It
is a function of loss of phosphate in the urine;loss in sweat; loss
during hemorrhage and loss during dialysis.

</content>
</topicdoc>

<topicdoc>
<name> Mass - PO4P5Definition </name>
<label> Definition </label>
<content>

Mass is the calculated amount of phosphate at any given time.
It is the change in phosphate integrated over time.

</content>
</topicdoc>

<topicdoc>
<name> Target[PO4--] - PO4P6Definition </name>
<label> Definition </label>
<content>

The Target[PO4--] is the initial plasma concentration of phosphate.
Target[PO4--] = Initial Mass/ECFV.Vol

</content>
</topicdoc>
