<name> LM_Glucose Release - Overview </name>
<label> Overview </label>
<content>

Hepatic gluconeogenesis and glycogenolysis are the two
processes responsible for liver glucose production.
The changes in hepatic glucose ouput are largely mediated
by changes in insulin and glucagon and by changes in
plasma glucose itself.
