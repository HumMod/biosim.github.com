Using mass balance, gas enters
the pulmonary artery at a rate
of capillary concentration times
ventilated flow plus venus
concentration times shunt flow.

Gas leaves the pulmonary artery
at a rate of total flow times
mixed concentration.

We solve here for mixed concentration.

[Cap] * VentFlow + [V] * Shunt
[A] = ------------------------------
Total Flow

Trap for 0 flow. If there is no flow,
the conc is 0.
