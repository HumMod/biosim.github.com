ADHPump.Setting

Definition

When the ADHpump switch is turned on, the model
user can set infusion rates using the sliding bar
feature in the ADH pump display box. Rates of infusion
may be increased from 0 in increments of 1, up to
10nG/Min and from 10 in increments of 10 up to 1000nG/Min.

Physical Units

[Plasma] pG/mL
Plasma Mass nG
Fluxes nG/Min
Stored Mass uG

Notice that micro (10^-6), nano (10^-9) and pico (10^-12)
gram units are used.

Concentration Units

ADH concentrations have been expressed in

pG/mL
pMol/L
uU/mL

The ADH pool is [ADH]*ECFV. Typical [ADH] is 2.0 pg/ml
and ECFV is 15,000ml.
2.0pG/ml*15,000ml = 30,000pG mass or 30nG.

A typical concentration is 2.0 pG/mL; 1.8pMol/L or 0.8uU/mL
To change from pG to pMol multiply by 0.922
To change from pG to uU, multiply by 0.4
