BetaPool.EpiPart

Definition

Since beta receptors see both epinephrine and norepinephrine,
the blood concentration of epinephrine and norepinephrine
do not quantitate either's effect on beta receptors. In the model
NE and Epi contribute equally to beta agonism and normal plasma
concentrations of NE and Epi are mapped to a beta agonism of 1.0.
This is done by using two constants NESCALE and EPISCALE to
calculate NEPart & EpiPart respectively.

NEPart = NEScale*[NE]
EpiPart = EPISCALE*[Epi]
