CreatineCells.TotalMass

Definition

Total mass is the creatine pool.
Total Mass = CreatineSkeletalMuscle + OtherMass

Concentration Units

Skeletal muscle has a creatine concentration of
4mG/G Wet Weight. Since skeletal muscle mass is 40%
of body weight, skeletal muscle creatine mass is
calculated as 4.1 * 27 = 111G.

Other Mass = 17G

Total Mass = 17 + 111 = 128G
