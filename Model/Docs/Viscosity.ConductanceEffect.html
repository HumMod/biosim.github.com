Viscosity.ConductanceEffect

Overview

Conductance is a measure of the blood flow through
a vessel for a given pressure difference. It is the
exact reciprocal of resistance. Since viscosity affects
resistance to flow, viscosity also affects conductance.
As viscosity increases, conductance decreases.

Physical Units

The viscosity of normal blood is about 3 times as great
as the viscosity of water.
