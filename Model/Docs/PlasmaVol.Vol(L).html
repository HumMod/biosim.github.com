PlasmaVol.Vol(L)

Definition

Vol is the calculated plasma volume at any given time.
Vol(L) is the plasma volume expressed in Liters
Vol(L) = Vol/1000.00

Overview

About 60% of the blood volume is plasma.
In the average adult male (70Kg) this is
approximately 3 Liters. This percentage varies
considerably depending on gender, weight, and
other factors.

Physical Units

Plasma Volume mL or L
Gain/Loss mL/Min

Concentration Units

Normal Plasma Volume is 3000mL
Gain 3.58mL/Min
Loss 3.55mL/Min
