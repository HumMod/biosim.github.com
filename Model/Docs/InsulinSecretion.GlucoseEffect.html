InsulinSecretion.GlucoseEffect

Overview

The earliest measurable response to declining plasma glucose
concentrations is suppression of insulin secretion. Insulin
secretion virtually ceases during hypoglycemia. The glycemic
threshold for suppression of insulin secretion is 83 mG/dL.

Schwartz NS, Clutter WE, Shah SD, Cryer PE. Glycemic
thresholds for activation of glucose counterregulatory
systems are higher than the thresholds for symptoms.
J Clin Invest. 79: 777 - 781, 1987.

Cryer PE. Glucose Counterregulation: prevention and correction
of hypoglycemia in humans. Am J Physiol 264: E149 - E155, 1993.

Blackard WG, Nelson NC. Portal and peripheral vein immunoreactive
insulin concentrations before and after glucose infusion.
Diabetse 19: 302-306, 1970.
