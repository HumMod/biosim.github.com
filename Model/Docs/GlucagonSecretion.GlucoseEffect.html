GlucagonSecretion.GlucoseEffect

Overview

Glucose has the ability to modulate its own metabolism by
the liver, independent of any change in insulin concentration.
When insulin and glucagon are at basal levels, hyperglycemia
inhibits endogenous glucose output and increases tissue glucose
uptake.
