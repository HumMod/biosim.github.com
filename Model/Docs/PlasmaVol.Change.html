PlasmaVol.Change

Definition

Changes in plasma volume are defined by three variables:
The intial volume and the gain and loss of plasma volume.

Physical Units

Plasma Volume mL or L
Gain/Loss mL/Min

Concentration Units

Normal Plasma Volume is 3000mL
Gain 3.58mL/Min
Loss 3.55mL/Min
