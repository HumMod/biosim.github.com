NaPool.Change

Definition

Changes in the sodium (Na+) pool or mass are a function of gains
and losses in sodium over time. Gains can occur from sodium in
the diet via reabsorption from the GI lumen; from intravenous
administration or from transfusion. Losses in sodium can occur in
urine; from sweating; from hemorrhage and during dialysis
