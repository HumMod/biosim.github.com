<topicdoc>
<name> SANode-Overview </name>
<label> Overview </label>
<content>

The SA node is a small flattened ellipsoid strip of
specialized muscle about 3mm wide, 15mm long and 1 mm
thick located in the superior posterolateral wall of
the right atrium immediately below and slightly lateral
to the superior vena cava.
The SA Node controls the rate of beat of the entire heart.
