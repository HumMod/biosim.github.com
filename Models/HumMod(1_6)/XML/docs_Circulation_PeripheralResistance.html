<topicdoc>
<name> PeripheralResistance - Physical Units </name>
<label> Physical Units </label>
<content>

Total Peripheral Resistance(TPR) = Systemic Arterial Pressure(AP)
minus Right Atrial Pressure(RAP) divided by Cardiac Cutput(CO)
TPR = (AP - RAP)/CO

Units are mmHg/(mL/Min).


</content>
</topicdoc>

<topicdoc>
<name> PeripheralResistance - Concentration Units </name>
<label> Concentration Units </label>
<content>

A typical value is 0.017mmHg/(ml/Min)


</content>
</topicdoc>

<topicdoc>
<name> PeripheralResistance - Overview </name>
<label> Overview </label>
<content>

Resistance is the impediment to blood flow in a
vessel. Total peripheral resistance is the
resistance to flow of the entire systemic circulation.

</content>
