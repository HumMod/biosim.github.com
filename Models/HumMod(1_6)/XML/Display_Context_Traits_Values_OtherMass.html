<line> To calculate initial body weight, the body is </line>
<line> divided into three compartments. </line>
<line> </line>
<line> Adipose Tissue (Fat) </line>
<line> Skeletal Muscle </line>
<line> Other Mass (All other tissue, including blood) </line>
<line> </line>
<line> Default (male) initial values are </line>
<line> </line>
<line> Adipose Tissue - 16.6 kG </line>
<line> Skeletal Muscle - 27.4 kG </line>
<line> Other Mass - 28.5 kG </line>
<line> </line>
<line> Temperature control also uses 3 compartments, but </line>
<line> they are different. </line>
<line> </line>
<line> Core Mass </line>
<line> Skeletal Muscle </line>
<line> Skin </line>
</infobutton>
