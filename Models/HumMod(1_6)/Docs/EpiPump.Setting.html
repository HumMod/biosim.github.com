EpiPump.Setting

Overview

The Epi Pump enables the model user to change
[Epi] levels by infusing Epi at different rates.
Data is taken from a variety of human studies
relating infusion rates to plasma concentrations
of epinephrine.

When the switch is turned on by the model user,
the rate is the level which is set using the
sliding bar in the pump edit box.

Physical Units

[Plasma] pG/mL or nMol/L
Plasma Mass nG or pG

milli = 10^-3
micro = 10^-6
nano = 10^-9
pico = 10^-12

Pump units are nG/Min

Concentration Units

Epinephrine (C9 H13 N O3) has a molecular weight
of 183.

Typical plasma concentration of epinephrine is
40pG/mL or 0.22nMol/L. (Multiply by 0.00546 to
convert pG/mL to nMol/L).
