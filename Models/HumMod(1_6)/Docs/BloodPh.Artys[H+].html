BloodPh.Artys[H+]

Definition

Artys[H+] is the hydrogen ion concentration of
arterial blood.

Physical Units

Units for H+ are Eq/L

Concentration Units

Normal arterial [H+] is 40nEq/L (0.00000004 Eq/L).
