NEPool.InitialMass

Definition

The initial mass is the starting amount of NE.
Since the plasma concentration of NE (the target[NE])is
240pG/mL and the volume of distribution for epinephrine is
the ECFV (15,000 mL), the typical initial mass
is 3600 nG (0.24*15,000).

Initial Mass = Target[NE]*ECFV.Vol

Physical Units

[Plasma] pG/mL or nMol/L
Plasma Mass nG or pG
Pump units are nG/Min

milli = 10^-3
micro = 10^-6
nano = 10^-9
pico = 10^-12

Concentration Units

weight of 169. Typical plasma concentration of
NE is 240pG/mL or 1.42nMol/L (Multiply by 0.00592
to convert pG/mL to nMol/L
