PlasmaVol.Loss

Definition

Loss is defined as negative changes in plasma volume.

Overview

Loss in plasma volume may occur in several
different areas:excretion by the kidney;
the peritoneum space;the capillaries;
the pulmonary system. Loss can also occur as
a result of hemorrhage and hemodialysis.

Physical Units

Plasma Volume mL or L
Gain/Loss mL/Min

Concentration Units

Normal Plasma Volume is 3000mL
Gain 3.58mL/Min
Loss 3.55mL/Min
