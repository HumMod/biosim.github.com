BloodPh.ArtysPh

Definition

It is customary to express H+ concentration of
blood on a logarithm scale, using pH units.
pH is related to the actual H+ concentration
by the following formula:

pH = log 1/[H+] = -log[H+]

ArtysPh is the arterial blood pH

Concentration Units

Normal value for arterial blood pH is 7.4

Cavaliere F, Antonelli M, Arcangeli A, Conti G, Pennisi MA,
Proietti R. Effects of acid-base abnormalities on blood
capacity of transporting CO2: adverse effect of metabolic
acidosis. Intensive Care Med 28: 609 - 615, 2002.
