HgbProps.CO2NORM

Definition

CO2NORM is a constant and is defined as the arterial
partial pressure of carbon dioxide under normal
physiological conditions.

It has a value of 40mm Hg.

Douglas AR, Jones NL, Reed JW. Calculation of
whole blood CO2 content.
J Appl. Physiol. 65: 473 - 477, 1988
