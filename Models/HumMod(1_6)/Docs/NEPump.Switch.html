NEPump.Switch

Overview

The NE Pump enables the model user to change
[NE] levels by infusing norepinephrine at different
rates. Data is taken from a variety of human studies
relating infusion rates to plasma concentrations
of norepinephrine.

When the switch is turned on, the model user sets the
rate by changing the setting using the slide bar.
rates can be increased in 100 unit increments.

Physical Units

[Plasma] pG/mL or nMol/L
Plasma Mass nG or pG
Pump units are nG/Min

milli = 10^-3
micro = 10^-6
nano = 10^-9
pico = 10^-12

Concentration Units

Norepinephrine (C8 H11 N O3) has a molecular
weight of 169. Typical plasma concentration of
NE is 240pG/mL or 1.42nMol/L (Multiply by 0.00592
to convert pG/mL to nMol/L
