PhBlood.H+

Definition

H+ is the hydrogen ion concentration in blood.

Physical Units

Units for H+ are Eq/L

Concentration Units

Normal values for H+ are as follows:
Arterial Blood: 40nEq/L
Venous Blood: 42nEq/L
Cell: 100nEq/L
