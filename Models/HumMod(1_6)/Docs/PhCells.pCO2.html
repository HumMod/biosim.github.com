PhCells.pCO2

Definition

pCO2 is the partial pressure of carbon dioxide

Physical Units

Units for pCO2 are mmHg

Concentration Units

Normal values for pCO2 are as follows;

Arterial pCO2 40mmHg
Venous pCO2 45mmHg
Cell pCO2 43mmHg
