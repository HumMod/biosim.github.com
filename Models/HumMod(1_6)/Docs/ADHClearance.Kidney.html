Overview

ADH is cleared from the pool by the liver and the kidneys.
With severe hemorrhage and hypotension, it is likely that the
clearance of vasopressin from the circulation will be reduced
because of decreased blood flow to the kidneys and splanchnic
viscera, the organs resposible for the removal of vasopressin
from the circulating blood.
