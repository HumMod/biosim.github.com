Overview

Renin in the kidney resides in two compartments:
a free compartment, comprising 10% of the total
and a granule compartment, comprising 90% of
the total amount.

Renin synthesis adds to the free compartment and
renin secretion takes from it. There is also a
shuttle between the free renin compartment and
the granule compartment.
