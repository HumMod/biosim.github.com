InsulinStorage.Mass

Definition

The insulin mass is the calculated amount of insulin present
in the beta cells of the pancreas.

Concentration Units

Physical Units

Units for insulin pool are mU.
Units for [plasma] are uU/mL.

uU * 0.04167 = nG
uU * 0.007241 = pMol


An international unit (U) is 1/24 mG.
Molecular weight is 5754.
