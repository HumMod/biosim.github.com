Definition

K is the time constant for renin clearance.
K is calculated from the half life of renin using
the following formula:

e^-kt1/2 = 0.5.
-kt1/2 = In(0.5)
k = -In(0.5)/t1/2 or
k = 0.69/t1/2

Since the half life of renin is approximately 42.75 minutes,
k = 0.69/42.75 = .0.161
