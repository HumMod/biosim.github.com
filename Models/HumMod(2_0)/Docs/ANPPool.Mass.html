ANPPool.Mass

Definition

Mass is the calculated amount of ANP at any
any given time. Change in the ANP pool or mass is a
function of both gains and losses in ANP over time.
Gains may occur from increases in ANP secretion rate,
from infusion of ANP using the ANP pump feature of HumMod.
Changes in the pool are calculated in the model using two
mathematical variables, F1 and F2, which integrate gains and
losses over time. A Backward Euler integral is used in this
calculation because it gives a more stable solution set.
