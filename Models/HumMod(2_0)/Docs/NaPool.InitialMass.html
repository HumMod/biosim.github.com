NaPool.InitialMass

Definition

The initial mass is the starting amount of sodium. Since the
plasma concentration is 144mEq/L (the Target[Na+]) and the
volume of distribution is the ECFV (15,000mL), then the
initial mass or circulating pool is 2160.

Initial Mass = Target[Na+]*ECFV.Vol
