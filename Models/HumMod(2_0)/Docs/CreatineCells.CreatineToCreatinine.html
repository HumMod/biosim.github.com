CreatineCells.CreatineToCreatinine

Overview

Creatinine is formed by nonenzymatic dehydration of
creatine and creatine phosphate within striated, smooth and
cardiac muscle. The rate of creatinine production in normal
subjects has been measured and was found to be 1.7% of the
creatine pool.

In HumMod, the creatine pool or mass is the sum of skeletal
muscle creatine mass and creatine in smooth & cardiac muscle,
collectively known as other mass.

Crim MC, Calloway DH, Margen S. Creatinine metabolism in men:
creatien pool size and turnover in relation to creatine intake.
J Nutr 106: 371 - 381, 1975.
