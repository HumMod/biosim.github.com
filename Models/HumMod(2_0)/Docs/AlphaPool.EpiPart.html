AlphaPool.EpiPart

Definition

Since alpha receptors see both epinephrine and
norepinephrine, the blood concentration of epinephrine
and norepinephrine do not quantitate either's effect on
alpha receptors. In the model NE and Epi contribute equally
to alpha agonism and normal plasma concentrations of NE and
Epi are mapped to an alpha agonism of 1.0.
This is done by using two constants NESCALE and EPISCALE to
calculate NEPart & EpiPart respectively.

NEPart = NEScale*[NE]
EpiPart = EPISCALE*[Epi]
