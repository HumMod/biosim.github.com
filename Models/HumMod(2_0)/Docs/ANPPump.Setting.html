ANPPump.Setting

Definition

When the ANPPump switch is turned on, the model
user can set infusion rates using the sliding bar
feature in the ANP pump display box. Rates of infusion
may be increased from 0, in increments of 10, up to
100pMol/Min and from 100, in increments of 100, up to
1000pMol/Min.

Physical Units

[Plasma] pG/mL or pMol/L
Fluxes pMol/Min

Concentration Units

ANP is a 28 amino acid protein with a
molecular weight of 3060

A typical concentration is 20pMol/L
1pMol/L = 3pG/mL

Normal ANP Secretion Rate is 67pMol/min.
Clearance Rate is 67 pMol/Min
Right Heart Secretion Rate: 41pMol/Min and
Left Heart Secretion Rate: 26pMol/min
