BetaPool.NEPart

Definition

Since beta receptors see both epinephrine and norepinephrine,
the blood concentration of Epi and NE do not quantitate their
effect on beta receptors. In the model NE and Epi contribute
equally to beta agonism and normal plasma concentrations of
NE and Epi are mapped to a beta agonism of 1.0. This is done
by using two constants NESCALE and EPISCALE to calculate NEPart
and EpiPart respectively.

NEPart = NESCALE*[NE]
EpiPart = EPISCALE*[Epi]
