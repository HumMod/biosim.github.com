CreatininePool.[Creatinine]

Definition

[Creatinine] is the plasma concentration of creatinine.
It can be expressed as mG/dL or uMol/L

Physical Units

[Plasma] mG/dL or uMol/L
mG * 0.00884 = mMol
mMol * 113.1 = mG
Mass mG
Clearance mG/min

milli = 10^-3
micro = 10^-6

Concentration Units

Creatinine (C4 H7 N3 O) has a molecular weight
of 113.12.

Typical plasma concentration is 0.82 mG/dL or
72.5 uMol/L

Creatinine excretion is 1.26dL/Min
