ANPSecretion.RAPEffect

Definition

RAPEffect is a multiplier of right base secretion and is used to
calculate Right Rate, the secretion rate of ANP from the right
atrium. It's value is calculated from a curve relating right atrial
transmural pressure to ANPSecretion. Data is taken from a variety of
sources listed below.

Dog Data
Metzler CH, Lee ME, Thrasher TN, Ramsay DJ. Increased right or left
atrial pressure stimulates release of atrial natriuretic peptides in
conscious dogs. Endocrinology 119: 2396 - 2398, 1986

Shin Y, Lohmeier TE, Hester RL, Kivlighn SD, Smith MJ Jr.
Hormonal and circulatory responses to chronically controlled
imcrements in right atrial pressure.
Am J Physiol 261: R1176 - R1187, 1991

Lohmeier TE, Shin Y, Rienhart GA, Hester RL. Angiotensin and ANP
secretion during chronically controlled increments in atrial
pressure. Am J Physiol 266: R989 - R996, 1994.

Human Data
Raine AE, Erne P, Burgisser E, Muller FB, Bolli P, Burkart F,
Buhler FR. Atrial Natriuretic peptide and atrial pressure in patients
with congestive heart failure.
New England J of Medicine 315: 533 - 537, 1986.

Richards AM, Cleland JG, Tonolo G, mcIntyre GD, Leckie BJ, Darfie HJ,
Ball SG, Robertson JI. Plasma alpha natriuretic peptide in cardiac
impairment. Br Med J 293: 409 - 412, 1986.
