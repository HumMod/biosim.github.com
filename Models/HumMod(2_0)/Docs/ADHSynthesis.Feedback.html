ADHSynthesis.Feedback

Definition

ADHSynthesis.Feedback is a multiplier of base secretion and
is dependant on the Slow Mass storage level. As the Slow Mass
levels decrease, synthesis increases.

Sachs H, Share L, Osinchak J, Carpi A. Capacity of the
neurohypophysis to release vasopressin.
Endocrinology 81: 755 - 770, 1967.

North WG. Chapter 4: Biosynthesis of vasopressin and neurophysins.

Gash DM, Boer GJ. Plenum Press New York and London 1987.
