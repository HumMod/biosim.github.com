CreatineCells.K

Definition

K is the rate constant for the conversion of creatine
to creatinine. Creatine exits muscle and is converted
to creatinine at a very steady rate constant of
0.0169/Day. Converting from G to mG/Min, this rate constant
becomes 0.0619 * 1000/1440 = 0.012. The units are (mG/Min)/G.

Since the molecular weight of creatine and creatinine are
131.15 and 113.12 respectively, the ratio is 0.86.

Therefore K = 0.012 * 0.86 = 0.010

Crim MC, Calloway DH, Margen S. Creatine metabolism in
men: urinary creatine and creatinine excretions with
creatine feeding. J. Nutr 106; 371 - 381, 1975.
