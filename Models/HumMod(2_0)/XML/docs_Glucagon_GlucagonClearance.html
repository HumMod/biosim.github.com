Glucagon infusion at 3 nG/kG/Min increased plasma glucagon
from 100pg/mL to 400pg/mL. From this data, we can calculate
that a secretion rate of 0.75nG/Min in a 75Kg subject will
raise plasma glucagon by 1.0pG/mL.

(3nG/kG/min in a 75Kg subject = 225nG/Min
225nG/Min raises plasma glucagon by 300pG/mL
75nG/Min raises plasma glucagon by 100pG/mL
0.75nG/Min raises plasma glucagon by 1.0pG/mL)

At a plasma glucagon level of 70pg/mL (continous drip),
the glucagon secretion rate is therefore 52.5 nG/Min

Assuming an ECFV of 15,000 mL, the glucagon mass would be
70 pG/mL * 15,000 mL = 1050nG

Degradation K is therefore 52.5 nG/Min divided by 1050 nG
52.5/1050 = 0.05 /Min
