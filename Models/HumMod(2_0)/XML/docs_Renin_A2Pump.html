<topicdoc>
<name> A2Pump - Overview </name>
<label> Overview </label>
<content>

The A2Pump enables the model user to change
[A2] levels by infusing angiotensinII at different
rates. Data is taken from a variety of human and
animal studies relating infusion rates to plasma
concentrations of A2.

When the switch is turned on, the model user sets the
rate by changing the setting using the slide bar.

Dog Data
Reid IA, Brooks VL, Rudolph CD, Keil LC. Analysis of the
actions of angiotensin on the central nervous system of
conscious dogs. Am J Physiol 243: R82 - R91, 1982.

Human Data
Oelkers W, Brown JJ, Fraser R, Lever AF, Morton JJ,
Robertson JI. Sensitization of the adrenal cortex to
angiotensin II in sodium-deplete man.
Circ Res 40: 69 - 77, 1974.

Deheneffe J, Cuesta V, Briggs JD, Brown JJ, Fraser R,
Lever AF, Morton JJ, Robertson JI, Tree M. response
of aldosterone and blood pressure to angiotensin II
infusion in anephric man. Effect of sodium deprivation.
Circ Res 39: 183 - 190, 1976.

Cachovan M, Brod J, Bahlmann J, Sippel R, Celsen B,
Hundeshagen H. The effect of intravenous angiotensin II
on the peripheral circulation with particular reference
to its bearing on general haemodynamics.
Clin Sci & Mol Med 51: 219s - 221s, 1976.

Oelkers W, Schoneshofer M, Schultze G, Bauer B. Effect of
prolonged low-dose Infusions of ile5-Angiotensin II on
blood pressure, aldosterone and electrolyte excretion in
sodium replete man. Klin Wochenschr 56: 37 - 41, 1978.

</content>
</topicdoc>

<topicdoc>
<name> Setting - DefinitionA21 </name>
<label> Definition </label>
<content>

When the A2Pump switch is turned on, the model
user can set infusion rates using the sliding bar
feature in the A2Pump display box. Infusion rates are
on a logarithmic scale. Rates of infusion may be
increased from 0, in increments of 10, up to 100;
from 100 in increments of 100 up to 1000; and from
1000 in increments of 1000, up to 10,000 nG/Min.

</content>
</topicdoc>

<topicdoc>
<name> A2 - Concentration Units </name>
<label> Concentration Units </label>
<content>

A typical A2 plasma concentration is 10 pG/ml
(recumbent level).
To change from pG to pMol multiply by 0.956
1.046 pG/mL = 1.0 pMol/L
