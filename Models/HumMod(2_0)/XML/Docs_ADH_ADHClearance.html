<topicdoc>
<name> ADHClearance - Overview </name>
<label> Overview </label>
<content>

Infusion studies examining ADH clearance rates in both
experimental animals (dogs and rats) and in human
subjects indicate that ADH is cleared mainly by the
kidneys, the liver and to a lesser extent by other tissues.
Total clearance is therefore the sum of these three
individual clearances. Individual clearances are calculated
from blood flow, plasma concentration of ADH and elimination
rate constants for extraction in the individual organs.
During severe hemorrhage and hypotension, clearance of vasopressin
from the circulation will be reduced because of decreased blood
flow to the kidneys, the liver and the other tissues responsible
for the removal of vasopressin from the circulating blood.

<topicdoc>
<name> Kidney - DefinitionADHC1 </name>
<label> Definition </label>
<content>

Kidney clearance is the rate of ADH cleared from the
kidney. It is a function of kidney blood flow, ADH
plasma concentration and the time constant K for
renal extraction of ADH.

</content>
</topicdoc>

<topicdoc>
<name> KidneyK - DefinitionADHC2 </name>
<label> Definition </label>
<content>

Kidney K is the time constant for the renal extraction
of ADH. Kidney K has a value of 0.00059

</content>
</topicdoc>

<topicdoc>
<name> Liver - DefinitionADHC3 </name>
<label> Definition </label>
<content>

Liver clearance is the rate of ADH cleared from the
liver. It is a function of GI blood flow, ADH
plasma concentration and the time constant K for
liver extraction of ADH.

</content>
</topicdoc>

<topicdoc>
<name> LiverK - DefinitionADHC4 </name>
<label> Definition </label>
<content>

Liver K is the time constant for the hepatic extraction
of ADH. Liver K has a value of 0.0005

</content>
</topicdoc>

<topicdoc>
<name> Other - DefinitionADHC5 </name>
<label> Definition </label>
<content>

Other clearance is the rate of ADH cleared from the
other tissues. It is a function of other tissue blood
flow, ADH plasma concentration and the time constant K for
other tissue extraction of ADH.

</content>
</topicdoc>

<topicdoc>
<name> OtherK - DefinitionADHC6 </name>
<label> Definition </label>
<content>

OtherK is the time constant for the clearance of ADH from
other tissue. OtherK has a value of 0.00108.

</content>
</topicdoc>

<topicdoc>
<name> Total - DefinitionADHC7 </name>
<label> Definition </label>
<content>

Total clearance is the sum of kidney, liver and other
tissue clearances.

</content>
