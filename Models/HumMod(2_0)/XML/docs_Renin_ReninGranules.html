<topicdoc>
<name> ReninGranules - Overview </name>
<label> Overview </label>
<content>



Renin in the kidney resides in two compartments:
a free compartment, comprising 10% of the total
and a granule compartment, comprising 90% of
the total amount.

Renin synthesis adds to the free compartment and
renin secretion takes from it. There is also a
shuttle between the free renin compartment and
the granule compartment.

Park CS, Malvin RL, Murray RD, Cho KW. Renin
secretion as a function of renal renin content
in dogs. Am J Physiol 234: F506 - F509, 1978.

Travis RH, Ricanati ES Temporal pattern of
secretion of renin in the hemorrhaged dog.
Endocrinology 111: 373 - 379, 1982.

Katz SA, Malvin RL. Secretion of newly synthesized
renin. Endocrinology 111: 201 - 207, 1982
