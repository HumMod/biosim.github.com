1pMol/L = 3pG/mL

Normal ANP secretion rate is 67pMol/min.
The right heart secretes 41pMol/Min and the left heart 26pMol/min

<topicdoc>
<name> ANPPump - Overview </name>
<label> Overview </label>
<content>

The ANPPump enables the model user to change
[ANP] levels by infusing atrial natriuretic peptide at different
rates. Data is taken from a variety of human and animal studies
relating infusion rates to plasma concentrations
of ANP.

ANP is a potent natriuretic peptide involved in hormonal
regulation of extracellular fluid volume and blood pressure
homeostasis. It is secreted by the atrial walls of the heart
in response to stretch, which can occur from excess blood volume.
ANP acts on the kidneys to cause small increases in glomerular
filtration rate (GFR) and decreases in sodium reabsorption
by the collecting ducts.

Dog Data
Maack T, Marion DN, Camargo MJ, Kleinert HD, Laragh JH, Vaughan ED Jr,
Atlas SA. Effects of auriculin (atrial natriuretic factor) on
blood pressure, renal function, and the renin-aldosterone
system in dogs. Am J Med 77: 1069 - 1075, 1984.

Burnett JC Jr., Granger JP, Opgenorth TJ. Effects of synthetic
atrial natriuretic factor on renal function and renin release.
Am J Physiol 247: F863 - F866, 1984.

Granger JP, Opgenorth TJ, Salazar J, Romero JC, Burnett JC,Jr.
Long-term hypotensive and renal effects of atrial natriuretic
peptide. Hypertension 8(6 Pt 2):II-112 - II-116, 1986.

Kivlighn SD, Lohmeier TE, Yang HM, Shin Y. Chronic effects of
a physiological dose of ANP on arterial pressure and renin release.
Am J Physiol 258: H1491 - H1497, 1990.

Human Data
Cody RJ, Atlas SA, Laragh JH, Kubo SH, Covit AB, Ryman KS,
Shaknovich A, Pondolfino K, Clark M, Camargo MJ. Atrial
natriuretic factor in normal subjects and heart failure
patients. Plasma levels and renal, hormonal, and hemodynamic
responses to peptide infusion. J Cli Invest 78: 1362 - 1374, 1986.

Yandle TG, Richards AM, Nicholls MG, Cuneo R, Espiner EA, Livesey JH.
Metabolic clearance rate and plasma half life of alpha-human atrial
natriuretic peptide in man. Life Sciences 38: 1827 - 1833, 1986.

</content>
</topicdoc>

<name> Switch - DefinitionANP2 </name>
<label> Definition </label>
<content>

When the ANPpump switch is turned on, the model
user can set infusion rates using the sliding bar
feature in the ANPPump display box.

</content>
</topicdoc>

<topicdoc>
<name> Setting - DefinitionANP1 </name>
<label> Definition </label>
<content>

When the ANPPump switch is turned on, the model
user can set infusion rates using the sliding bar
feature in the ANP pump display box. Rates of infusion
may be increased from 0, in increments of 10, up to
100pMol/Min and from 100, in increments of 100, up to
1000pMol/Min.

</content>
